Getting Started
If you haven't already, download and install Adobe Photoshop on your computer. You can choose between a subscription-based Creative Cloud version or a standalone version, depending on your preferences.
2
Don't be overwhelmed
Familiarize yourself with the interface when you launch Photoshop, take some time to explore the interface, and become familiar with its different panels, menus, and tools. The main toolbar on the left side contains various tools for editing and creating images.
Start with the essential tools in Photoshop, such as the Move tool, Marquee tool, Lasso tool, Brush tool, and Eraser tool. These tools allow you to make selections, move objects, and paint your images.

Now for the challenge
After familiarizing yourself with the interface, it's time to follow the main tutorial. You will be learning how to change the color of an object, swap faces smoothly, and design a simple poster for a pizza parlor.
5
Additional Photoshop Hacks
Here are some tips for using Photoshop effectively:
  • Layer masks enable you to selectively hide or reveal parts of a layer without permanently erasing pixels.
  • Smart Objects are non-destructive layers that preserve the original content of an image or object. They allow for easy scaling, editing, and applying filters without losing image quality.
  • Blending modes determine how layers interact with each other. Experiment with different blending modes to create unique effects.
